Microsoft Cloud Community Leaderboard Wiki            


For Content, we have awarded points based on the following criteria:

·       Docs Point = Suggestion * 15 + Accepted Suggestion * 60

·       One Suggestion = 15 points

·       One Approved Suggestion = 60 points


Scenario: Sandra is a customer who submits a PR with one suggestion.
By default, she gets 15 points and if her suggestion has been accepted
after being reviewed, she will get 60 additional points
for a total of 80 points.


For Software, we have awarded points based on the following criteria:

·       Software Point = Suggestion word *2 + Voted String * 2 + Accepted translation word * 8

·       1 suggested word = 2 points

·       1 approved word = 8 points

·       Voting other users’ translation = 2 points per vote


Scenario: Jeff is a customer that reviews a MT translated sentence of 10 words,
he makes edits accordingly and submits it. By default, he gets 20 points.
If his translation gets voted and approved, he gets 80 additional points
for a total of 100 points. He can also review someone’s suggested words, vote for it and get additional 2 points.
