Microsoft Cloud Community Leaderboard Wiki            

For Content, we have awarded points based on the following criteria:

·       Docs Point = Suggestion * 15 + Accepted Suggestion * 60

·       One Suggestion = 15 points

  • One Approved Suggestion = 60 points

 

Scenario: Sandra is a customer who submits a PR with one suggestion. By default, she gets 20 points but if her suggestion has been accepted after being reviewed, she will get 80 additional points for a total of 100 points. Otherwise, if her suggestion is not accepted, she will only receive 20 points.

 

For Software, we have awarded points based on the following criteria:

 

·       Software Point = Suggestion word *2 + Voted String * 2 + Accepted translation word * 8

·       1 suggested word* = 2 points

  • 1 approved word = 8 points
  • Voting other users’ translation = 2 points per vote

 

Scenario: Jeff is a customer that reviews a MT translated sentence of 10 words, he makes edits accordingly and submit it. By default, he gets 20 points. If his translation gets voted (twice by other users) and approved, he gets 80 additional points for a total of 100 points. Additionally,he can review someone’s suggested words, vote for it and get additional 2 points.
